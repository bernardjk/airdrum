Welcome to AirDrummer!

How to play:

1. Click Start and allow this page to use your camera.

2. When you are ready click the "Record" button and make some gestures with your hands.
(At the top-right of the screen you will find a guide of the possible sounds and the corresponding hand gesture in the bottom left of the image.)

3. After all of the boxes have filled up go ahead and click the "Play All Sounds" button to hear your tune.

4. Click on the clear button and start a new beat.
